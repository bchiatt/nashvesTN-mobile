Help the Homeless

About

NashvesTN helps non-profit organizations increase their community impact by engaging the Millennial Generation.

Our mobile app makes it easier for social advocates to donate directly to homeless individuals in their community. Through a participating non-profit organization, homeless individuals can set up a profile to tell their story, share their skills & aspirations, & provide updates to advocates.

Using a simple QR code linked to their profile (put it on a sticker, badge, or business card!), homeless individuals can share their profile with the community . Advocates scan the QR code, which pulls up the homeless individual's profile. Within a few clicks, advocates can learn more about the individual in need, make a donation, & save the profile their list.
